About NACCS League

Welcome to the North American Casual Counter Strike League! We are a community-driven league dedicated to providing a platform for casual Counter-Strike 2 players to come together, compete, and enjoy the game.

Our mission is to create an environment suited for players who enjoy a chill environment and enjoy the competitive aspect of CS without the pressure of committing so much time to practices and etc. Best enjoyed with a cold beer and good friends.
